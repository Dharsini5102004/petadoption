Cuteness overload: bunny edition.

Rabbits are a perfect pet for those who don't have time for daily walks but still want a dog-like companion. They can be easily litter box trained and are known for their funny habits, crepuscular schedules, and health benefits. They are also great for city dwellers, have long lifespans, and provide a sense of security. Adopting a rabbit is a great decision, as they are the third most abundant adoptable pet after dogs and cats.
