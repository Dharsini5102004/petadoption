Pet bird species vary greatly, with no single diet suitable for all. A balanced diet is crucial for preventing common problems like moulting disorders, respiratory diseases, growth abnormalities, and poor reproduction. Owners should focus on protein, fat, carbohydrate, vitamin, and mineral levels rather than solely on seed ratios. Feeding bird seed in the correct ratio can lead to deficiencies and low protein levels.
